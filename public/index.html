Frequently asked questions

  • What do we need to get started?

    All we need is a space that can hold 8 to 10 people that we can reserve 3 times a week before work, typically Monday, Wednesday, Friday 7:30 am – 9:00 am.

    We’ll provide the facilitation, coffee, shakes, and other materials necessary!

  • What makes the Team Morning Upgrade unique?

    In addition to our intakes, assessments, morning routines (including delicious coffee and shakes!), we offer complimentary accountability check-ins and offline coaching to your employees during the engagement.

  • What are the benefits of a morning routine?

    Morning routines are followed by the most accomplished athletes, entrepreneurs, and creatives, including Bill Gates, Benjamin Franklin, and Oprah and are a powerful way to fulfill your most important daily needs — hydration, movement, connection, focus, and creation.

  • What do work sessions consist of?

    It's up to you what you want to work on, either work-related or personal! However, we find that tackling projects you've been putting off, difficult emails, and personal tasks that would otherwise distract you during work are some of the most effective tasks.
